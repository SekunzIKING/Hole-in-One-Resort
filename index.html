About Our Resort

Portugal's Premier Golf Destination

Nestled along the stunning Portuguese coastline, Hole in One Golf Resort offers an unparalleled golfing experience. Our resort combines championship-level courses with luxury accommodations and exceptional service to create unforgettable golf vacations.

With three world-class golf courses, including the famous Sea View course, our resort caters to golfers of all skill levels while providing breathtaking views and challenging play.
